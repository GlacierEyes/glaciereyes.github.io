Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Glacier Eyes

What makes your thumbnails different?

+

Our thumbnails are strategically designed based on data-driven insights specific to your niche. We don't just create visually appealing designs; we craft thumbnails that are optimized for maximum CTR (click-through rate) and audience engagement. Each thumbnail is meticulously crafted to tell a story that compels viewers to click.

How long does the process take?

+

Typically, we deliver thumbnails within 24 hours, depending on the complexity and number of thumbnails ordered. For rush orders, we offer expedited services at an additional fee. We'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific needs and our current workload.

Do you offer channel audits or strategy sessions?

+

Yes! In addition to thumbnail design, we offer comprehensive channel audits and content strategy sessions. These services provide actionable insights to improve your overall channel performance, audience growth, and revenue potential. Our experts analyze your existing content, audience demographics, and competitive landscape to develop a customized growth strategy.

How do I know your thumbnails will work for my niche?

+

Before creating any designs, we conduct thorough research on your specific niche, analyzing top-performing thumbnails, audience preferences, and current trends. Our team has experience across diverse content categories, and we tailor our approach to match what works in your particular space. Additionally, we track the performance of our thumbnails and make adjustments as needed to ensure optimal results.

What do you need from me to get started?

+

To begin, we'll need basic information about your channel, including your content style, target audience, and any existing brand guidelines. For specific thumbnails, we'll request details about the video content, any key elements you want to highlight, and any reference images you may have. The more information you provide, the better we can tailor our designs to your needs.

Do you offer revisions on thumbnails?

+

Absolutely! All our thumbnail packages include up to 2 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product. If you need additional revisions beyond what's included in your package, we offer them at a nominal fee. Our goal is to create thumbnails that you're excited to use and that deliver results for your channel.
