GRAFF TAPES

Reimagine an overlooked analogue medium through a contemporary brand and visual identity, designed to reconnect younger audiences with the physicality of cassette tapes.

Graff Tapes is a conceptual brand that reintroduces cassette culture in a digital-first era, celebrating tactility, nostalgia, and personal expression. The project blends the raw, expressive language of graffiti with the warmth and imperfection of analogue sound, positioning cassette tapes as cultural objects rather than obsolete technology.

By combining bold visual systems with modern applications, the project reframes cassette tapes as collectible, expressive, and relevant once again, demonstrating how design can revive forgotten formats and give them new meaning in contemporary culture.
