Thrumpton Golden Cod

Reimagine a local fish and chip shop through a contemporary brand identity that balances tradition and modern appeal.

Thrumpton Golden Cod is a brand identity concept developed to refresh a familiar local business with a clearer, more considered visual language.

The result is a cohesive and adaptable identity that modernises the brand without losing its approachable, neighbourhood feel, demonstrating how thoughtful design can elevate everyday local businesses.

Project Overview

My role: Graphic Designer
