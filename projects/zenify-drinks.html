ZENIFY DRINKS

Digital-first campaign with clean impact.

Zenify is a social media campaign concept designed around freshness, clarity, and movement. The objective was to create a repeatable grid and content system that stays flexible across product drops.

The project includes post templates, motion-ready story layouts, and a visual style that keeps product storytelling at the center.
