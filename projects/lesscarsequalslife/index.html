LESS CARS EQUALS LIFE

This project explores how graphic design can question car-dominated urban spaces and highlight their impact on health, wellbeing, and everyday life.

Using bold typography and a reduced visual language, Less Cars Equals Life communicates the social and environmental benefits of reducing car dependency.

The project translates complex urban issues into clear, accessible messages, positioning design as a tool for awareness, discussion, and positive change within contemporary cities.

Project Overview
