ISTD

Respond to an ISTD brief by exploring the technical, cultural, and contextual role of typography through research-led design.

This project explores advanced typographic layouts and editorial design for the International Society of Typographic Designers (ISTD) brief. The focus is on clarity, structure, and expressive use of type to communicate a chosen theme.

The outcome balances academic rigour with creative expression, presenting the typeface in a format that is both informative and visually compelling.
